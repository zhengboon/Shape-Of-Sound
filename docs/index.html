Shape of sound

Background

This project involves inserting a marble into a track, detecting its color, and playing a sound based on the color of the marble. It is a 2-player instrument, where the marble playing is a conversation between two people, where one responds to what the other person has played. The instrument is split in the center, with 2 sides forming a track
